## ğŸ¨ Design Principles

### 1. **Empathy First**
- Acknowledge customer frustrations
- Use apologetic language when appropriate
- Show understanding and care

### 2. **Visual Hierarchy**
- Headers (###) for main topics
- Bold (**) for critical information
- Emojis for visual landmarks
- Bullet points for easy scanning

### 3. **Action-Oriented**
- Clear next steps
- Multiple contact options
- Easy-to-find important details (phone numbers, timeframes)

### 4. **Conversational Tone**
- Friendly and approachable
- Not overly formal
- Use of "we," "you," and "let's"

### 5. **Complete Information**
- Answer the question fully
- Provide additional helpful context
- Offer alternative solutions

### 6. **Closing with Care**
- Friendly sign-off
- Invitation for further questions
- Positive ending emoji
